Radiator Repair service

Kerry's Radiators Mainly Provide Radiator services, this includes the Selling, Repairing and the Reconditioning/Re-coring of old and damages radiators. we service many different types of radiators, these include: Small too Large Radiators of Commercial/Plant Vehicles.

If your in need of a radiator quickly you can buy one from us and we'll install it for you too. We can repair damaged radiators to get them working again and if there too badly damaged we can recondition them giving them a new lease of life.

Selling Radiators

If you bring us a damaged radiator that cannot be fixed then you can buy one directly from us, we may already have your radiator in stock however depending on the model of the radiator you need we may need to order one in which may take some time.
You don't need a broken radiator to buy one, if you are just in need of a new radiator we'll supply you with one. The price of the radiator can vary from make and model but we guaranty you'll get a good deal.

Repairing Radiators

When you bring us your damaged radiator there is a good chance we should be able to fix it. Light damage can be patched up and repaired in a reasonable amount of time, even heavy damage can some times be repair dependent on its overall condition, however some damaged radiators will be too damaged to be repaired but may be reconditioned instead.

Reconditioning/Re-coring/Custom Radiators

Reconditioning a radiator is what we are known for as only a very small amount of company's have the skilled employees to do this. We will take your old and damaged radiator and give it a new lease of life. This can involve striping it down and replacing the damaged sections with new or re-coring the entire radiator. Here at Kerry's Radiators we provide the service to create custom radiators, this means if you need a bit extra added onto your radiator or need a specialy spaded radiator we can still help you.
