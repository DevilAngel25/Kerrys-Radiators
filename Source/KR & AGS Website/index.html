Welcome to Kerry's Radiators

Kerry's Radiators has been in business for many years. They continue to provide motor vehicle services which include: Pre-MOT's, Motor vehicle Repair and Repairing, Selling and Reconditioning Old and damaged Radiators. Kerry's Radiator's have over 30 years of experience in the trade and believe in a Reliable, Friendly and Personal touch in the work they perform. Their aim is to give an exceptional quality of service for the customer and their needs.

Kerry's Radiators do more than repair and MOT standard Motor vehicles, they also sell, repair and recondition Commercial/Plant Machinery (Radiators and Coolers). Kerry's Radiators are one of the few which will take your old radiator and recondition it, this is very helpful if you own a classic or ageing vehicle and cannot find a replacement radiator.
