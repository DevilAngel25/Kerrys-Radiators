The Pre-MOT service

Alan's Garage Services Provide Pre-MOT services, a Pre-MOT is a easier way to get your vehicle an MOT. We can pick up your car from your home and take it to the garage where it will receive the MOT, While your car is reciveing the MOT we can provide you with a certesy car to help you continiue your day as normal, (Unfortunatly at certian times the certesy car may not be avalibe but this should stop you from enquireing about it.) after this we'll bring the car back to you with all of the necessary papers.
We can also give your car a Full Service after your car has had its MOT ,this can be done if You think its necessary or if You could save time or money in the future.
Unfortunately we do not handle any Motor bikes or any vehicles at Class 7 or above.

The cost of a Pre-MOT can vary depending on the type of vehicle and the garage at which it receives the MOT,
but don't be afraid of phoning us to get a estimated quote of the price so you can compare the cost with other garages.
